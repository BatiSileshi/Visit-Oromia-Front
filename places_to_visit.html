Places to visit

Sof Omar Cave

Location: Bale Zone
Is the longest cave in Ethiopia at 15.1 kilometres (9.4 mi) long. When surveyed in 1972, it was the longest cave in Africa. The caves are known for their many pillars, particularly in the "Chamber of Columns".

Bale Mountains National Park

Location: Bale
Is a national park in Ethiopia. The park encompasses an area of approximately 2,150 square kilometres in the Bale Mountains and Sanetti Plateau of the Ethiopian Highlands.

Awash National Park

Location: Oromia
Is a national park in Ethiopia. Spanning across the southern tip of the Afar Region and the northeastern corner of the East Shewa Zone of Oromia, this park is 225 kilometers east of Finfinne.

Arsi Mountains National Park

Location: Arsi Zone
The park encompasses the Arsi Mountains, which are part of the Ethiopian Highlands. Extend northeast to southwest through the park, and form the southern wall of the African Rift Valley.
